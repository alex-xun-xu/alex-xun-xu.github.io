I am a Research Fellow working with Prof. Cheong Loong Fah in Visual Interactive Media Lab of National University of Singapore since Sep 2016. I received a PhD degree from Computer Vision Group in the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary, University of London, jointly supervised by Prof. Shaogang Gong and Dr. Tao Xiang. I was also working closely with Dr. Timothy Hospedales .

Before joining the Computer Vision Group in QMUL I received my B.Eng in Automation from Sichuan University and then had spent 2 years pursuing M.Sc in 3D Vision Lab at Sichuan University supervised by Prof. Yusheng Liu and Prof. Kai Liu.

My research interests include motion segmentation, structure from motion, zero-shot learning and surveillance scene understanding.
