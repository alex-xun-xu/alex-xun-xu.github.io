I am currently a PhD student within the Computer Vision Group in the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary, University of London, jointly supervised by Prof. Shaogang Gong and Dr. Tao Xiang. I am also working closely with Dr. Timothy Hospedales .

Before joining the Computer Vision Group in QMUL I received my B.Eng in Automation from Sichuan University and then had spent 2 years pursuing M.Sc in 3D Vision Lab at Sichuan University supervised by Prof. Yusheng Liu and Prof. Kai Liu.

Currently I am interested in Activity Recognition using Transfer Learning. I am now developing framework learning collective behaviours especially in learning motion patterns from a scene captured by static camera. In this framework motion pattern knowledge learned from one scenario can be transferred to the recognition of motion patterns in another scenario.
