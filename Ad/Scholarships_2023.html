PhD/Postgraduate/Undergraduate Scholarship Opportunities with A*STAR
We have scholarships covering PhD, postgraduate and undergraduate levels. If you are interested in doing research internship or pursuing PhD degree in computer vision and machine learning, in particular, domain adaptation, semi-supervised learning, weakly supervised learning, active learning and 3D point cloud deep learning, please contact Dr. Xun Xu xux@i2r.a-star.edu.sg
  • We are looking for highly motivated international students who wish to do PhD in A*STAR, NUS, NTU, SUTD (PhD degree will be conferred by NUS, NTU or SUTD). We provide full scholarship under the SINGA program (check your eligibility).
  • We also welcome international full-time PhD students who wish to do research attachment/internship (1-2 years with A*STAR). Full scholarship under the ARAP program will be provided during the attachment/internship period in Singapore (Check your eligibility)
  • We also welcome students from China who are eligible for CSC scholarship to do research attachment (1-2 years with A*STAR). Full scholarship under the CSC program (Check your eligibility)
  • We provide scholarship for talented foreign postgraduate/undergraduate students who wish to do research attachment/internship (2-6 months with A*STAR) or prepare to apply for PhD under the SIPGA program (Check your eligibility).
